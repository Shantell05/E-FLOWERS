Meet Our Founder

Sarah Johnson - Founder & Head Florist

Frequently Asked Questions

How long has Eleve Flowers been in business?

Eleve Flowers was founded in 2020 and has been serving the Johannesburg community with premium floral arrangements for over 4 years.

Do you source flowers locally?

Yes! We work with local South African flower growers to ensure the freshest blooms while supporting our local economy.

What makes Eleve Flowers different?

Our attention to detail, personalized service, and commitment to creating unique, handcrafted arrangements sets us apart.
