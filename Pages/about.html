About Eleve Flowers

Meet Our Founder

Sarah Johnson - Founder & Head Florist

Sarah Johnson founded Eleve Flowers in 2020 with a passion for bringing beauty into everyday life through floral artistry. With over 10 years of experience in floral design, Sarah's vision was to create a floral boutique that combines elegance with personal touch.

Our Story

Eleve Flowers was born from Sarah's childhood memories of her grandmother's garden. The name "Eleve" (meaning "elevated" in French) reflects our commitment to creating floral arrangements that elevate special moments and transform ordinary days into extraordinary memories.

What started as a small home-based business has grown into Polokwane's premier floral destination, serving weddings, events, and daily floral needs with the same passion and attention to detail that inspired our beginning.
